AI for Financial Inclusion

A website that provides information on the role AI has in improving financial equity. Take action by calling or emailing your local Senator to support H.R.4503 - the AI Training and Expansion Act.

Evergreen: A Gardening Game

In "Evergreen," players explore the stages of plant growth, gathering raindrops in a watering can and dispelling cloud cover to unveil the sun. The challenge lies in strategically nurturing their garden, utilizing natural elements for optimal growth. The game unfolds as players guide their garden through its enchanting journey of development.

Shooting Star Collection Game

In the "Shooting Star Collection Game," players aim to accumulate a score of 10 by collecting stars, with each missed star leading to a reduction in score. The objective is to navigate through the night sky and gather stars using the telescope.
